
# User Roles & Permissions

## 1. SUPER_ADMIN
The platform owner.
- **Goal:** Health of the SaaS ecosystem.
- **Views:** Global MRR, active tenants, subscription management.

## 2. PROFESSIONAL
The service provider (Tenant).
- **Goal:** Manage business operations.
- **Views:** Appointment calendar, Service CRUD, client list, AI insights.

## 3. CLIENT
The end-user / patient.
- **Goal:** Book services efficiently.
- **Views:** Directory of professionals, Booking flow, personal appointment history.
